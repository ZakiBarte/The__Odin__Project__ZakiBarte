Classic Meat and Cheese Lasagna

A rich, hearty lasagna with layers of seasoned ground beef, creamy ricotta, melted mozzarella, and a slow-simmered tomato sauce, all tucked between tender lasagna noodles and baked to golden, bubbly perfection.

This classic lasagna is the ultimate comfort food. Each bite delivers a savory blend of herby tomato meat sauce, smooth ricotta, and stretchy, golden-baked mozzarella. The edges are crispy and caramelized, while the inside stays moist and layered. It's filling, aromatic, and deeply satisfying — perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Egg
  • Fresh basil or parsley
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil

List of Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles – 12 pieces
  • Ground beef – 500g
  • Onion – 1 medium, chopped
  • Garlic – 3 cloves, minced
  • Crushed tomatoes – 800g can
  • Tomato paste – 2 tbsp
  • Ricotta cheese – 450g
  • Mozzarella cheese – 400g, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese – 100g, grated
  • Egg – 1 large
  • Fresh basil or parsley – ¼ cup, chopped
  • Salt – 1 tsp
  • Black pepper – ½ tsp
  • Olive oil – 2 tbsp

Steps (Unordered)

  • Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  • Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat olive oil in a pan. Add onion and garlic, sauté until soft.
  • Add ground beef, cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and fresh herbs.
  • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  • Layer: noodles → ricotta mixture → meat sauce → mozzarella. Repeat.
  • Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  • Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  • Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Ordered Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles; drain and set aside.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
  4. Brown ground beef; drain fat.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, pepper; simmer 10 minutes.
  6. Mix ricotta, egg, and herbs in a bowl.
  7. Layer meat sauce at bottom of baking dish.
  8. Add noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, mozzarella; repeat layers.
  9. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  10. Cover with foil; bake 25 minutes.
  11. Uncover; bake 15 minutes more.
  12. Rest 10 minutes before serving.
