Creamy Garlic Mushroom Pasta

Description

This creamy garlic mushroom pasta is a comforting, savory dish with a rich and velvety sauce. The mushrooms are sautéed until golden and slightly crisp at the edges, releasing an earthy, umami depth. Fresh garlic infuses the butter and cream with a warm, aromatic pungency that mellows into sweetness. Each strand of pasta is coated in the smooth, cheesy sauce, while a sprinkle of parsley adds a fresh, peppery contrast. The result is a balanced, satisfying meal — indulgent yet simple.

    Ingredients

  • Pasta (fettuccine or spaghetti)
  • Fresh mushrooms (cremini or button)
  • Garlic cloves
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Olive oil

    Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil and butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add sliced mushrooms and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Pour in heavy cream and stir. Simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Stir until smooth.
  7. Toss in the cooked pasta and mix well.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley. Serve hot.
